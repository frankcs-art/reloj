## 2025-01-31 - Fearless Customization with Reset Pattern
**Learning:** In highly customizable interfaces (like watch face builders), users are more likely to experiment when they have a clear, "safe" way to return to a known good state (the default theme).
**Action:** Always include a "Reset" or "Restore Defaults" button with immediate visual confirmation when implementing complex customization panels.
