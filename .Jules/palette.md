## 2025-05-14 - Accessible Buttons and Glanceable Text
**Learning:** In watch face designs, developers often prioritize aesthetics over accessibility, leading to unreadable 6-7sp text and non-semantic interactive elements.
**Action:** Always ensure small labels are at least 9sp/9px for readability and use semantic <button> or Modifier.semantics for all interactive components to support screen readers and keyboard navigation.
